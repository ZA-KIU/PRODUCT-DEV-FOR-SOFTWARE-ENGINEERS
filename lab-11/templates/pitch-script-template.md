## DELIVERY TIPS

**Voice:**
- Speak slowly (you'll naturally speed up when nervous)
- Pause after important numbers
- Vary your tone (don't monotone)
- Project to back of room

**Body Language:**
- Stand, don't sit
- Face audience (not screen)
- Use gestures to emphasize points
- Make eye contact

**Pacing:**
- If ahead: Pause, add detail to stories
- If behind: Cut less important details
- Never rush the demo
- Never skip the ask

**Energy:**
- Start strong (first 30 seconds matter)
- Show passion when describing problem
- Be confident on business model
- End with energy
