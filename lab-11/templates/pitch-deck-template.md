## ğŸ“ Deck Design Guidelines

### Typography
- **Headings:** 36-48pt, bold
- **Body text:** 24-28pt minimum
- **Captions:** 18-20pt minimum
- **Max fonts:** 2 (one for headings, one for body)

### Layout
- **Max bullets per slide:** 3-5
- **Max words per bullet:** 10
- **Whitespace:** Don't cram, let slides breathe
- **Alignment:** Left-align text, center images

### Color
- **Background:** White or very light gray
- **Text:** Dark gray or black
- **Accent color:** Pick ONE brand color, use sparingly
- **Contrast:** Ensure text is readable from 20 feet

### Images
- **Quality:** High resolution (not pixelated)
- **Size:** Large enough to see details
- **Attribution:** Not needed for your own screenshots
- **Stock photos:** Avoid if possible (use real product/team)
