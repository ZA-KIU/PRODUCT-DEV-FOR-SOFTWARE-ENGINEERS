## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|------------|--------|---------------------|
| Can't recruit enough participants | Medium | High | - Start outreach early<br>- Have 5+ backup channels<br>- Offer incentives if needed |
| Team member unavailable | Medium | Medium | - Cross-train on tasks<br>- Document everything<br>- Have backup owners |
| Experiments take longer than planned | Medium | High | - Build buffer time<br>- Prioritize #1 experiment<br>- Be ready to extend timeline |
| Data collection fails | Low | High | - Test tracking before launch<br>- Have manual backup<br>- Export data daily |
| Results are inconclusive | Medium | Medium | - Design follow-up experiments<br>- Talk to users for context<br>- Document what we learned |
