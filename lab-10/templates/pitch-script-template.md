## üé§ Speaking Notes & Stage Directions

### Delivery Tips

**Energy & Pacing:**
- Start with HIGH energy (hook needs punch)
- Slow down for key numbers/stats
- Speed up slightly during feature lists
- Return to moderate pace for closing

**Vocal Variety:**
- [EMPHASIZE] words that matter
- [PAUSE] before important points
- Lower voice for serious stats
- Raise voice for exciting traction

**Body Language:**
- Maintain eye contact with evaluators
- [GESTURE] to slides when referencing them

- Stand still during demo (don't block screen)
