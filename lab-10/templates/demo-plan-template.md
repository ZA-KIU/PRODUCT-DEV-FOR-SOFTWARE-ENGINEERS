## üìä Success Criteria

### What Makes a Great Demo

**Our demo will be successful if:**
- [ ] Clearly shows the problem being solved
- [ ] Demonstrates key value proposition
- [ ] Completes in 60-90 seconds
- [ ] No technical glitches
- [ ] Audience understands what they saw
- [ ] Connects to our pitch narrative

**Red flags to avoid:**
- [ ] Taking longer than 90 seconds
- [ ] Showing features without context
- [ ] Technical jargon
- [ ] Broken or buggy product
- [ ] Empty states or missing data
- [ ] No clear user story
